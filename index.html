About Me

Hi, I'm Landon Pillar — a data-driven college student pursuing a Bachelor of Applied Science in Data Analytics and Programming. I specialize in turning raw data into actionable insights and compelling visual stories.

My toolkit includes Power BI, Tableau, Excel, SQL, and Python, and I'm constantly learning new tools to sharpen my analytical edge. Whether it's uncovering trends, cleaning messy datasets, or building dashboards that tell a story, I enjoy solving real-world problems with data.

I’m actively building a portfolio that showcases my ability to not just analyze data, but communicate it clearly. I'm passionate about the intersection of data, technology, and decision-making — and I'm excited to bring that energy to teams that value curiosity and clarity.
